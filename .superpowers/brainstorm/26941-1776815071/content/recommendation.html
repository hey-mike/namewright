Recommendation: B → A (Launch Spike, then SEO Engine)

Not one or the other — sequence them. The spike funds and validates the content engine.

Why not A alone

SEO takes 3–6 months to compound. With no audience and no revenue, you'll lose motivation before the flywheel spins. You need early signal — paying customers, testimonials, and proof the product works — before you can write credible content.

Why not C

Partners have leverage. If Indie Hackers or a newsletter drops you, your distribution disappears overnight. Too much risk as the foundation of a solo business.

The sequenced play

Month 1–2
Polish + Launch
Add payments (Stripe), improve UI, ship on Product Hunt + HN Show HN + r/startups. Goal: 20–50 paying customers, 5–10 testimonials.
Month 3–5
Content Engine
Write 15–20 SEO articles using real customer questions. Topics: "how to name a startup", "trademark search guide", "best domain alternatives". Tool embedded in each article.
Month 6+
Compound + Expand
Organic traffic → freemium conversions → paid reports. Reinvest in data quality (real WHOIS, USPTO live). Consider agency tier once volume justifies it.

Expected outcome at 12 months

500–2000 monthly organic visitors, 2–5% paid conversion = 10–100 reports/month at $19 = $2,280–$22,800 ARR. Not life-changing, but a validated, compounding asset.
